Challenge #1 - Favorite Top 10 Website Assignment

The Matrix : Sci-Fi/Action
Release year: 1999
Critic concensus: Though The Matrix ultimately overdoses on gloom-and-doom grunge, over-elaborate cool and especially running time, it's just clever enough to enable [the Wachowskis] to nix the sophomore jinx following 1996's Bound.

Starring: Keanu Reeves


Matrix Reloaded: Sci-Fi/Action
Release year: 2003
Critic concensus: The whole film may be more than a bit suspect cerebrally, with dime-store philosophy, potted mysticism and cut-price otherworldliness, but visually it gives full value as a virtuoso workout for your senses.

Starring: Keanu Reeves


Matrix Revolutions: Sci-Fi/Action
Release year: 2003
Critic concensus: An impressive closing to the cycle..

Starring: Keanu Reeves


Matrix Ressurections: Sci-Fi/Action
Release year: 2021
Critic concensus: A movie that is in direct conversation with itself and what came before, and while the routes it takes to get there are sledgehammer blunt at times, the points it raises are wild.

Starring: Keanu Reeves


John Wick Chapter 1: Action/Mystery/Thriller
Release year: 2014
Critic concensus: Stylish, thrilling, and giddily kinetic, John Wick serves as a satisfying return to action for Keanu Reeves -- and what looks like it could be the first of a franchise..

Starring: Keanu Reeves


John Wick Chapter 2: Action/Mystery/Thriller
Release year: 2017
Critic concensus:An adrenaline-pumping blockbuster polished to near perfection, save its sequel-baiting conclusion.

Starring: Keanu Reeves


John Wick Chapter 3: Action/Crime/Thriller
Release year: 2019
Critic concensus: Parabellum puts its stamp on what is the best action franchise of the past 20 years.

Starring: Keanu Reeves


John Wick Chapter 4: Action/Crime/Thriller
Release year: 2023
Critic concensus: Sensational stunts, rigorous physical blocking and camera choreography, judicious but satisfying gore, and an incongruous nod to Frogger—good stuff, Mr. Wick.

Starring: Keanu Reeves


Armageddon: Science Fiction
Release year: 1998
Critic concensus: For its targeted young audience, this is summer entertainment at its superficial, excessive best, a combination roller coaster ride and fireworks show, with doomsday as its funhouse theme.

Starring: Bruce Willis


Cutting Edge : Romance/Comedy
Release year: 1992
Critic concensus: The dialogue sings, the direction is clean, and there are nearly countless moments that still stand out as iconic today..

Starring: Moira Kelly and DB Sweeny
